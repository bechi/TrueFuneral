DIRECT CREMATION

Includes transfer, care of your loved one and the cremation itself. Ashes are provided to family in a basic urn or container.

WHAT YOU GET

Body transport, care, cremation, basic urn.

NOT INCLUDED

Body transport, cremation, basic container for ashes.

WHY YOU WOULD PAY MORE

Body transport, cremation, basic container for ashes.

IMMEDIATE BURIAL

Immediate Burial services include transport of body, body care, transport to cemetery, placement in burial plot, temporary marker.

WHAT YOU GET

Body transport, body care, transport to cemetery, placement in burial plot, temporary marker.

NOT INCLUDED

Service, casket, burial plot, opening & closing grave fee.

WHY YOU WOULD PAY MORE

Various casket options, funeral services, cemetery burial plots, etc.
