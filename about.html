TrueFuneral helps make one of life's toughest decisions easier.

Learn how

Losing a loved one is one of the most difficult times of your life. The pressure to find the right funeral service provider quickly can also make it one of the most stressful.

We have worked hard to provide the most accurate, comprehensive and unbiased information possible to empower you to make the very difficult decision of finding a funeral provider for your loved one.


To ensure the highest degree of accuracy, we have physically visited – in lieu of calling – each and every funeral home in your area to collect a physical General Price List.

Why did we do this? As a consumer, you deserve the best information to help you make the best decision for your needs — and that’s what we provide through True Prices found only on TrueFuneral.com.
