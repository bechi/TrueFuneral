What is Direct Cremation?

We are so sorry for your loss. Here is the process of Direct Cremation, explained in 8 simple steps.

1

ARRIVAL

Selected service provider arrives at location of deceased.

2

PROVISION

Explanation and provision of all necessary paperwork and permits.

3

AUTHORIZATION

Service provider secures all permits and Authorization of Cremation.

4

TRANSFER

Service provider transfers body to funeral home.

5

CARE

48-hour care of body at the funeral home.

6

CREMATION

Cremation occurs.

7

ASHES

Service provider places ashes into included basic urn.

8

RETURN

Family retrieves ashes from service provider.
