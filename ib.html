What is Immediate Burial?

All you need to know about Immediate Burial, explained in 9 simple steps.

1

ARRIVAL

Selected service provider arrives at location of deceased.

2

PROVISION

Explanation and provision of all necessary paperwork and permits.

3

AUTHORIZATION

Service provider secures all permits and Authorization of Burial.

4

TRANSFER

Service provider transfers body to Care Center.

5

CARE

Care of body at Care Center.

6

SELECTION

Family selects cemetery and purchases a burial plot; procures a casket of their choice.

7

TRANSFER

Transfer of body to selected cemetery.

8

BURIAL

Body is lowered into purchased burial plot with included limited graveside service.

9

MARKER

Temporary marker is placed at burial site.
